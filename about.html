About US

Its one of a kind

Cicada has been a cornerstone event at Atria Institute of Technology SINCE 2021. We have had thousands of participants over the years, who are passionate about pushing the boundaries.

We aim to inspire, innovate and collaborate students to explore new technologies, connect with like minded individuals and industry professionals, provide hands-on experiences and creative problem solving approaches.
